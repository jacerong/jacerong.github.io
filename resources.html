Datasets

Spammer Detection

This dataset contains a labeled collection of Twitter spammers and non-spammers who posted content related to the Colombia 2014 presidential election. To each user in the dataset, a feature vector was computed from the history of its 5, 10, 20, and 40 most recent tweets.

Sentiment Analysis and Voting Inference

This dataset is composed of two subsets of Spanish tweets and a third subset created by aggregating opinion polls. It was collected during the Colombia 2014 presidential election. Its goal is to enable developing approaches to sentiment analysis of Spanish political tweets and data-driven strategies for inferring voting in both the first round and run-off elections.
