Jhon Adrián Cerón-Guzmán

Data Scientist

About

Jhon Adrián Cerón-Guzmán designs and develops technologies that help gain a deeper understanding of individuals' attitudes and behavior. To achieve this, his work focuses on combining, either from an empirical or a formal perspective, the capabilities of machine learning, natural language processing, and, broadly, data science with theories from a variety of fields of knowledge, ranging from social sciences and up to marketing and finance.

Over the last years, Jhon Adrián has acquired valuable skills from his career in both academia and industry. On the one hand, he has undertaken research in social media mining and natural language processing and their application in a political election. As a result, he has published several peer-reviewed papers on topics such as spammer detection, spell checking, and sentiment analysis and received the best paper award for one of his contributions. On the other hand, his work experiences have taught him to work independently and provided him with opportunities to develop leadership-related skills.
